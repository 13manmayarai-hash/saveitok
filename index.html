Questions
Frequently Asked
Is SaveItOk completely free? ▼
Yes. TikTok, Douyin, Instagram and Facebook downloads are completely free — no account, no payment, no hidden fees. The site is supported by advertising.
How do I download a TikTok video without a watermark? ▼
Paste the TikTok video link into the box above and click Download. SaveItOk automatically removes the TikTok watermark during processing.
What video quality options are available? ▼
Available qualities depend on the source video. We offer HD (1080p) for most platforms and an audio-only MP3 option for all supported sites.
Can I download Instagram Reels and Stories? ▼
Yes. SaveItOk supports Instagram Reels, Stories, and regular posts. For Stories, make sure to copy the link while the story is still live.
Is it legal to download videos? ▼
Downloading videos for personal offline viewing is generally acceptable in most countries. Redistributing, republishing, or monetizing downloaded content without the creator's permission may violate copyright laws and platform terms of service.
Why is my download not working? ▼
Make sure the video URL is complete and the video is publicly accessible. Private videos, age-restricted content, and region-locked videos cannot be downloaded. Try copying the link directly from the share button.
